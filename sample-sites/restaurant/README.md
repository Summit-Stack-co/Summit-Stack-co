# Juniper & Ash Kitchen - Image Checklist

This checklist matches the current restaurant sample page.

The page uses:
- 1 full-screen hero image
- 5 gallery images

Use this checklist to source image assets for `sample-sites/restaurant/`.

## Required Images

1. **Hero image**
   - **Purpose:** Full-screen first impression behind the headline and reservation CTA
   - **Content:** Plated food or restaurant interior with warm, dramatic lighting
   - **Recommended size:** `2600 x 1600` or larger
   - **Orientation:** Landscape
   - **Notes:** This is the most important image on the page. It should immediately set the tone.

## Gallery Images

2. **Plated dishes**
   - **Purpose:** Main gallery food image
   - **Content:** One or two beautifully plated dishes with texture and detail
   - **Recommended size:** `1600 x 2000`
   - **Orientation:** Portrait

3. **Restaurant interior**
   - **Purpose:** Dining room / atmosphere image
   - **Content:** Tables, lighting, banquettes, open room, or windows at night
   - **Recommended size:** `2000 x 1400`
   - **Orientation:** Landscape

4. **Bar / drinks**
   - **Purpose:** Cocktail or bar atmosphere image
   - **Content:** Drinks, bar counter, glassware, bottles, or bartender area
   - **Recommended size:** `1600 x 1200`
   - **Orientation:** Landscape

5. **Chef cooking**
   - **Purpose:** Kitchen action image
   - **Content:** Chef at the fire, plating, or cooking over flame
   - **Recommended size:** `1600 x 1200`
   - **Orientation:** Landscape

6. **Close-up textures**
   - **Purpose:** Editorial detail image
   - **Content:** Ingredients, fire, plating detail, char, herbs, sauce, or table texture
   - **Recommended size:** `1600 x 1200`
   - **Orientation:** Landscape or portrait

## Image Quality Standards

- High-quality food photography is essential
- Warm light is better than bright daylight
- Avoid sterile stock-food shots on pure white backgrounds
- The room should feel lived-in and atmospheric, not empty or corporate
- Slightly imperfect editorial framing is better than generic hospitality stock
- Darker shadows are fine, but the images should still feel warm and inviting

## File Naming Suggestions

- `hero-restaurant-interior.jpg`
- `gallery-plated-dishes.jpg`
- `gallery-dining-room.jpg`
- `gallery-bar-drinks.jpg`
- `gallery-chef-cooking.jpg`
- `gallery-closeup-textures.jpg`

## Implementation Notes

- Replace the hero image first
- Then replace the plated dishes and interior gallery images
- The bar, chef, and texture images finish the editorial feel
- Use `.jpg` or `.webp` for all photos
