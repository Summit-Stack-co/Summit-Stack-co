About

Juniper & Ash is built around seasonal ingredients, open-fire cooking, and a space designed for long dinners.

The tone here is intimate, warm, and just a little smoky. It should feel like a restaurant brand with its own point of view, not a polished template.

Experience

Dinner service

Long-table energy, warm light, and a menu built to unfold over the course of the evening.

Private events

Gatherings that need something more personal, with room for conversation and a slower pace.

Chef’s tasting

A more guided experience centered around fire-led cooking and the season’s best ingredients.
