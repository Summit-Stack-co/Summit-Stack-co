# Silver Valley Injury Law - Image Checklist

This page currently uses a conversion-first layout that works without photos, but adding the right images will make it feel more real and locally credible.

Use this checklist to source image assets for `sample-sites/law-firm/`.

## Required Images

1. **Hero background image (highest priority)**
   - **Purpose:** Main above-the-fold visual behind headline and form
   - **Content:** Professional attorney-client consultation or attorney in office
   - **Style:** Natural lighting, professional wardrobe, serious but approachable
   - **Recommended size:** `2400 x 1400` (or larger)
   - **Orientation:** Landscape
   - **Notes:** Leave negative space for left text and right form overlay

2. **Practice area section support image (optional but recommended)**
   - **Purpose:** Can be used as subtle section texture or background accent
   - **Content:** Local courthouse exterior, city legal district, or professional office corridor
   - **Recommended size:** `1800 x 1200`
   - **Orientation:** Landscape

3. **Credibility/brand texture image (optional)**
   - **Purpose:** Soft background texture layer for strip/banner areas
   - **Content:** Neutral legal-themed texture (paper, desk surface, office detail)
   - **Recommended size:** `1600 x 900`
   - **Orientation:** Landscape
   - **Notes:** Keep low contrast so text remains readable

## Nice-to-Have Images

4. **Attorney portrait image**
   - **Purpose:** Trust element for later section enhancement
   - **Content:** Single attorney portrait, neutral office backdrop
   - **Recommended size:** `1200 x 1500`
   - **Orientation:** Portrait

5. **Team/office image**
   - **Purpose:** Secondary visual for social proof or firm culture block
   - **Content:** Small legal team in conference room
   - **Recommended size:** `1800 x 1200`
   - **Orientation:** Landscape

## Image Quality Standards

- No logos, watermarks, or branded marks from real firms
- No dramatic "gavel stock photo" cliches
- Avoid overly staged smiling stock photos
- Match tone: serious, dependable, local professional services
- Keep color grading warm-neutral to match current page palette

## File Naming Suggestions

Use predictable names so wiring is easy:

- `hero-attorney-consultation.jpg`
- `bg-courthouse-local.jpg`
- `bg-office-texture.jpg`
- `attorney-portrait.jpg`
- `team-conference.jpg`

## Implementation Notes

- Prefer compressed `.jpg` for photos and `.webp` variants when available
- Keep important subject matter out of top navigation overlap area
- Ensure text contrast remains strong after placing image overlays
